Official Bio

Jihang Dai (b. 2000), a native of Beijing, China, is currently pursuing his Master's degree in composition at Syracuse University, where he is a student of Nicolas Scherzinger and Loren Loiacono. He previously received his B.A. from Franklin and Marshall College, where his teachers included John Carbon and Julia Adams. While studying at Franklin and Marshall College, he was the recipient of the Nolt Award, the John Carbon Composition Contest Award, and Marshall Fellow. His works have been performed by performers such as himself (on the piano), Doris Hall-Gulati, Robin Plant, Guy McIntosh, Ana B. Martinez, and Syracuse University's “Contemporary Performance Lab.”
